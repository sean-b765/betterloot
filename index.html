This tool helps Rust server owners who have the Oxide plugin BetterLoot.
Here you can edit the loot tables for each container easily and search for items, without having to go through 4000 lines of text!

The containers without mouse-over image previews are not in Rust as of May 2021 (as they have no in-game prefabs)

The custom loot table used by BetterLoot is located at: /oxide/data/BetterLoot/LootTable.json
  1. Locate your LootTable.json file and make a backup of it just in case
  2. Import it via the button at the top of this page
  3. Perform your alterations
  4. Click 'Export file...' to download your new LootTable.json file, place it in your /data/BetterLoot/ folder, and reload Oxide!
